It took a little longer for One Piece to establish itself in the West than it did for some of the other great Shonen anime series. Once it did though, it's never once struggled to keep Western audiences engaged.

Neon Genesis Evangelion

There were plenty of great mecha anime series unveiled throughout the decade, but none have proven to be anywhere near as popular nor quite as enduring as Hideaki Anno's Neon Genesis Evangelion and its various spin-offs and big-screen adaptations.

COWBOY-BEPOP

Pokemon took the world by storm when it arrived on the scene in the mid-to-late nineties, with the TCG, video games, and anime all becoming incredibly popular almost overnight.
